HONK! Bradford

A free street-band festival in the heart of Bradford, hosted by The Peace Artistes. The last edition ran in 2025.

This page documents what HONK! Bradford was — the festival, the visiting bands, the special HONK! Band ensemble — for anyone who came to a year, or wants to know what they missed.

The last festival · 2025

The final HONK! Bradford ran 24 September – 8 October 2025, with bands from Spain, Germany, France, the Netherlands, Australia, the US and across the UK. The headline show was on Saturday 27 September in City Park, alongside 16 visiting streetbands.

The HONK! Band

A one-off massed ensemble of up to 100 players assembled for the 2025 festival, directed by Ken Field from HONK! Boston. Open to anyone 18+ with regular playing experience (drum / percussion beginners welcome). Eight rehearsals across September, free to take part, finishing with the City Park performance on 27 September.
